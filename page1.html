CHAT-ROOM (Beta)

Chatroom Rules:

 

Respect Others:

Treat everyone with kindness and respect.No harassment, bullying, or hate speech.

Offensive language, discriminatory remarks,or personal attacks will not be tolerated.

 

Stay On Topic:

Keep conversations relevant to the chatroom’s purpose. Avoid spamming, trolling, or disrupting discussions.

No Personal Attacks:Criticize ideas, not people.Refrain from name-calling, insults, or personal arguments.

 

Protect Personal Information:

Do not share your own or others' private information (e.g., phone numbers, addresses, passwords).

Avoid asking others for personal information.

 

No Unauthorized Promotions:

Refrain from posting advertisements, promotions, or any unsolicited content without permission.

Sharing links to unrelated websites or services is not allowed.

 

Follow the Law:

Do not engage in illegal activities or share illegal content.

Discussions that encourage illegal activities are prohibited.

 

Use Appropriate Language:

Keep language clean and suitable for all participants.

Avoid excessive use of caps or symbols.

 

Privacy Warning:

Public Space: Remember, this chatroom is a public space.

Anything you post here may be visible to others.

 

Personal Information:

Do not share sensitive personal information, such as your address, phone number, or financial details.

Protect your privacy by keeping this information confidential.

 

Data Security:

Be cautious when sharing any personal details, even in private messages.

The chatroom cannot guarantee the security of your data.

 

Third-Party Links:

Avoid clicking on unknown links shared in the chatroom.

They may lead to phishing sites or contain malware.

 

No Guarantees:

While we strive to protect your privacy, we cannot guarantee complete confidentiality.

Use the chatroom responsibly and at your own risk.
